About

Story Board:

You wake up on the floor of a dark cellar. You don't remember your name, where you are, or how you got there.

OBJECTIVE:

Escape alive, don't get killed by any of the traps or monsters. Find out who you really are and why the maze will not ever let you leave this dark mess!

Plays:

Navigate to the main menu by pressing the spacebar on your keyboard, if you are new, select the "1" key to start a new game. If you are returning and want to resume your session, press the "2" key. When you have started the session, you may use the onscreen controls, or your keyboard to navigate. Along the course of the game there will be clues and items to find. By piecing them together, you may be able to defeat the maze!
